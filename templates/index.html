Welcome to cs2api!

To access all skins: cs2api/skins

To access skin by id: cs2api/skins/search?id=1

To access skin by name: cs2api/skins/search/n?name=Duality

To access list of all collections: cs2api/collections

To access specific collection: cs2api/collections/search/n?name=Revolution Case

to drop skin from collection/case: /cs2api/skins/drop/n?collection=Revolution Case
