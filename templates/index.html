Welcome to cs2api!

To access all skins: cs2api/skins

To access skin by id: cs2api/skins/search?id=1

To access skin by name: cs2api/skins/search/n?name=M4A1-S | Vaporwave

To access list of all collections: cs2api/collections

To access specific collection: cs2api/collections/search/n?name=The Gallery Collection

to drop skin from collection/case: cs2api/skins/search/n?name=Hybrid
